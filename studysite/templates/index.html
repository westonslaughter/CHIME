Welcome to the CHIME Study

California Health Impacts from Mining Exposure


We're looking into public health issues related to heavy metal pollutants from mining history in California's Northern Sierra region. The research is conducted by Nevada City based community environmental research non-profit Sierra Streams Institute, as well as partners at University of California San Francisco, and University of Arizona.

Interested? We are looking for women living in the Northern Sierra to participate!
